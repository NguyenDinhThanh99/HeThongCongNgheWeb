One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begin re-evaluating his prioreties in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance int the courts. The Street Lawer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm. The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
